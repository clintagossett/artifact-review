## Design & Tone Guidelines

### Language

- **Clear, not intimidating**: Use plain language where possible
- **Organized**: Use headers, bullets, short sections
- **Transparent**: Explain "why" behind key terms
- **Balanced**: Protect both parties fairly

### Structure

- **Table of contents**: Jump links at top
- **Definitions**: Define key terms early (in Section 1 or dedicated section)
- **Cross-references**: Link related sections
- **Visual breaks**: Use whitespace, formatting

### Trust Signals

- **Fair terms**: Avoid one-sided provisions
- **User-friendly**: Refund policy, cancellation terms
- **Transparent enforcement**: Clear consequences for violations
