---

## What are cookies?

Cookies are small pieces of data stored in text files on your computer or device when websites are loaded in a browser. They are used to remember information about your visit, such as your preferences and login status. Cookies may be set by the site you are visiting ("first-party cookies") or by third-party services that the site uses ("third-party cookies").

Related technologies such as web beacons (also called "pixels" or "tracking pixels") are small, transparent images embedded in web pages or emails that help us understand usage patterns. We may use these alongside cookies.

We use cookies for the following purposes:

- **To keep you logged in** and maintain your session as you navigate between pages.
- **To remember your preferences** and settings.
- **To understand how you use our Services** so we can improve them.
- **To process payments** securely through our payment processor.

## Types of cookies we use

### Essential cookies

These cookies are strictly necessary for the Services to function. Without them, you would not be able to log in, navigate the site, or use core features. You cannot opt out of essential cookies while using our Services.

| Cookie | Provider | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|----------|---------|----------|
| Session cookies | Clerk | User authentication and session management | Session / up to 7 days |
| CSRF tokens | Artifact Review | Protection against cross-site request forgery attacks | Session |

| Cookie | Provider | Purpose | Duration |

Some of our service providers may set their own cookies when you interact with their services through our platform. We do not control these cookies.

| Cookie | Provider | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|----------|---------|----------|
| Payment processing | Stripe | Secure payment processing, fraud prevention | Varies (see [Stripe's Cookie Policy](https://stripe.com/cookie-settings)) |

**Note:** We do not use advertising cookies. We do not serve ads on our platform, and we do not share your data with advertising networks.

## Managing cookies

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject cookies (other than essential cookies).

### Browser settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can usually find these in the "Options", "Settings", or "Preferences" menu of your browser. The following links may help:

- [Chrome](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647)
- [Firefox](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/cookies-information-websites-store-on-your-computer)
- [Safari](https://support.apple.com/guide/safari/manage-cookies-sfri11471/mac)
- [Edge](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-edge/delete-cookies-in-microsoft-edge-63947406-40ac-c3b8-57b9-2a946a29ae09)

Please note that disabling essential cookies may prevent you from using certain features of our Services.

### Do Not Track

Some browsers offer a "Do Not Track" (DNT) signal. Our Services currently do not respond to DNT signals, but we limit our tracking to the cookies described in this policy.
