## Your Rights Under the CCPA

As a California resident, you have the following rights:

- **Right to Know.** You have the right to know what personal information we collect, use, disclose, and sell/share. This notice and our Privacy Policy provide this information.
- **Right of Access.** You can request a copy of the specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you.
- **Right to Delete.** You can request that we delete the personal information we have collected from you, subject to certain exceptions.
- **Right to Correct.** You can request that we correct inaccurate personal information we maintain about you.
- **Right to Opt-Out of Sale/Sharing.** You have the right to opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal information. **We do not sell or share your personal information as defined under the CCPA, and we have not done so in the past 12 months.**
- **Right to Limit Use of Sensitive Personal Information.** We do not use or disclose sensitive personal information for purposes beyond those permitted by the CCPA, so no opt-out is needed.
- **Right to Non-Discrimination.** We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your CCPA rights. We will not deny you goods or services, charge you different prices, or provide a different level of quality because you exercised your rights.

## How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of the rights described above, you may:

- **Email us** at [privacy@artifactreview.com](mailto:privacy@artifactreview.com)
- **Update your account settings** directly within the Services

When you submit a request, we will need to verify your identity. At a minimum, we will verify your name and the email address associated with your account. If we cannot verify your identity, we may be unable to fulfill your request.

You may also designate an authorized agent to make a request on your behalf. To do so, we will require written authorization with a signature from the account holder.
