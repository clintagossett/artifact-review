## 5. Common Law Trademark Analysis

### Legal Context

**Common Law Trademark Rights** are established through:
1. **Actual use in commerce** - Regular and recurring business use
2. **Geographic scope** - Rights limited to area where mark is used
3. **Clear evidence** - Demonstrated connection between mark and goods/services

**Source:** [Common Law Trademark Rights - Griffith Barbee](https://griffithbarbee.com/common-law-trademark-rights-and-priority-of-use/)
